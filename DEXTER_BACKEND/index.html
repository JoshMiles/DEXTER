FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When will it be released?

Hopefully within the next two months or so. Apply for beta testing for early access!

Where do you get your data from?

We use Pokémon Go Hub to populate the Pokédex with Pokémon Go data. We also pull data directly from Pokémon Go's main game data. We use Pokémon Go Map to show all Pokéstop and Gym locations using an in-app browser directing straight to their site. The same applies to TheSilphRoad's Nest Atlas also.

Why is x Pokémon not showing yet?

If a new generation has been released, our team is work tirelessly to update our servers with the relevant Pokémon data. Thank you for your paitence!

Is the app offline?

Unfortunately, the app requires internet access to ensure all the data is kept up to date (e.g pokéstop locations, gym locations, pokémon data).
